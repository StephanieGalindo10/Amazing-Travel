About Me

My work explores the relationship between the tyranny of ageing and multimedia experiences. With influences as diverse as Wittgenstein and Joni Mitchell, new combinations are crafted from both mundane and transcendant dialogues. Ever since I was a child I have been fascinated by the ephemeral nature of the zeitgeist. What starts out as vision soon becomes debased into a manifesto of futility, leaving only a sense of decadence and the prospect of a new beginning. As intermittent forms become frozen through studious and academic practice, the viewer is left with a hymn to the limits of our future.
